Oatmeal

Description

Oats are great as a source of fiber and are relatively easy to cook, whether it is in a microwave or on the stovetop. Sweet or savory; they are incredibly versatile and oatmeal can be garnished with a variety of toppings of our liking.

Oatmeal is a staple breakfast item which I find can sustain me for a long period of time with stable energy levels which makes it a healthier choice compared to most cereals.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of oats of your choice
  • 2 cups of water
  • One pinch of salt
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • 1 tsp of chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp of honey
  • 2 tbsp granola or nuts (optional)

Steps (Saucepan)

  1. Bring the water to a boil.
  2. Add salt, oats, and chia seeds when water is at a boil. Stir frequently to prevent the oats from burning. Note: Please be careful of splatters when the oats are cooking. Reduce heat to medium or medium low, if needed.
  3. When the oats are fully cooked, place into a bowl. Add 1 tbsp of honey and mix. Top with the sliced banana.
  4. optional: Add granola or nuts for a bit of crunch.

I hope you enjoy this recipe. This is a very simple recipe and you can spice it up by adding chocolate chips or any other fruits of your choice as toppings. Some fruits that I would recommend are strawberries and blueberries. I sometimes also add some oatmilk to make it creamier.
